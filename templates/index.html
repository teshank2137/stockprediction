Here for demo I'm Using TCS.NS as stock name

  • To get buy sell condition for today: /predict/[stockname]
  • To get Current market price: /current/[stockname]
  • To get 1 week data: /week/[stockname]
  • To get Tommorow predicted Value: /rawpredict/[stockname]
  • To get full raw data for n days moving averages and all: /rawdata/[stockname]/[days]
Please use following StockNames only
  • TCS.NS
  • SBIN.NS
  • HDFCBANK.NS
  • RELIANCE.NS
